What We Do

Our Services

We provide comprehensive cleaning solutions for individuals, families, hotels, salons, and corporate clients across Nairobi.

Dry Cleaning
Expert solvent-based cleaning for your finest garments — suits, coats, silk, and delicate fabrics treated with utmost care to restore their original look and feel.
Laundry
Full wash, dry, and fold service for everyday clothing. We handle bulk loads efficiently with fast turnaround — fresh, clean clothes returned within 24 hours.
Ironing
Precision steam ironing for wrinkle-free, crisp results. Whether for office wear or special occasions, your clothes leave looking polished and presentable.
Duvet & Blanket Cleaning
Deep cleaning for duvets, blankets, and heavy bedding. We remove allergens, dust mites, and odors — leaving your bedding hygienic, fluffy, and fresh-smelling.
Curtain Cleaning
Professional curtain laundering with careful handling of fabrics, pleats, and linings. We restore brightness and eliminate dust — perfect for homes, offices, and hotels.
Carpet Cleaning
Deep extraction carpet cleaning to remove stains, dirt, and embedded grime. We revive the color and texture of your carpets, leaving them spotless and sanitized.
Sofa Set Cleaning
On-site or drop-in sofa and upholstery cleaning for fabric and leather sets. We remove stains, odors, and bacteria — restoring the freshness of your living spaces.
Corporate Uniforms
Bulk uniform cleaning and pressing for businesses, schools, and organizations. We maintain the professional appearance of your team's attire with consistent, high-quality results.
Wedding Gowns
Specialized bridal gown cleaning and preservation. We handle intricate lace, beading, and delicate fabrics with expert care — keeping your dress as stunning as your special day.
Graduation Gowns
Thorough cleaning and pressing of academic gowns, hoods, and regalia. We ensure you look impeccably sharp for your big graduation moment — every pleat in place.
Suits & Dresses
Careful dry cleaning and pressing of suits, blazers, and formal dresses. We protect linings, buttons, and structure so your formalwear always looks its very best.
Leather Jackets
Specialized leather cleaning and conditioning to remove grime while preserving suppleness and color. We restore the natural sheen and extend the life of your leather garments.
Priest Robes
Respectful, thorough cleaning of clerical robes, vestments, and liturgical garments. We treat every piece with the care and reverence these sacred garments deserve.
Sports Shoes
Deep clean and deodorize sports and casual shoes using safe cleaning agents. We restore whiteness, remove stubborn stains, and leave your shoes looking and smelling brand new.
Hotel Uniforms
High-volume uniform laundering and pressing for hospitality staff. We maintain consistency and professionalism in every piece — helping your hotel uphold a five-star appearance.
Salon & Barber Towels
Reliable bulk towel washing for salons and barbershops. We sanitize and restore fluffiness to your towels on a regular schedule — keeping your salon hygienic and professional.
